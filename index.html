Features

FireVsIce Custom Plugin

One of the biggest changes to factions this server brings is a custom made plugin called FireVsIce. On join, the player chooses a team. That player then is in what's called a "SuperFaction"; Fire or Ice. The adventure continues when the player finds a base of the opposite SuperFaction. Every so often, players can opt in (or out) of superfaction battles with high rewards, but high risks as well.

EipcWorldGen

The entire factions world is generated with EpicWorldGen, a plugin well known for its massive structures, enhanced biomes, and beautiful landscapes. The player is warmly welcomed in spawn, but in the wild, he/she has numerous enemies. The enhanced map should be used to your advantage to help yourself and hinder your opponents, making a challenging factions experience.

KitPvP

All builds on the server are created by a professional build team developed by the server founders, so all builds are clean, neat, and seamless. They populate the player's vision with professionalism and quality.
